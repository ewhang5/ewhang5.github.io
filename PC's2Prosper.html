PC's2Prosper

Because of the coronavirus pandemic, many families were hit hard, and the need for electronics increased. Thus, I came up with the idea of using electronics that others no longer needed. PC's2Prosper will create a website to connect businesses that no longer need certain electronics with schools in need. We will also refurbish the electronics before we deliver them to the schools.
